Challenges

This a site that can link to fitness tracking networks (Currently Strava) and syncs your data. This currently duplicates what you can already see in strava. I have built it around a challenges features (Which Strava later refreshed goals to be similar) that allows an admin to create challenges and for users to join them and track progress throughout the duration. Challenges currently supported are time, distance and elevation. They can be limited by activity type.
